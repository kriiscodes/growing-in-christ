# Growing in Christ — PRD

## Product Summary
Growing in Christ is a private, mobile-first web app designed for a single Christian community to grow spiritually through a structured weekly rhythm.

## Core Principles
- Not a social platform
- No likes, comments, streaks, or public metrics
- Focus on depth, consistency, and private growth
- Grace over performance

## Users
- Leader (admin)
- Members

---

## Weekly System

Each week is defined by time, not naming.

A week is identified by:
- start date
- end date

The meaning of the week comes from the **Weekly Word**, not a week title.

Each week contains:
- Weekly Word
- Prayer Focus
- Journey Check-in
- Saturday Takeaway
- Midweek Reflection

---

## Core Features

### 1. Dashboard (This Week)
A calm overview showing:
- current week (date-based)
- Weekly Word
- Prayer Focus
- Progress (Check-in, Takeaway, Reflection)
- Latest Announcement

---

### 2. Weekly Word
- scripture focus
- study content
- optional resources

This is the central teaching for the week.

---

### 3. Prayer

#### Group
- Weekly Prayer Focus

#### Personal
- Private prayer journal
- Mark prayers as answered

---

### 4. Journal
A private notebook for:
- reflections
- lessons
- scripture notes
- personal thoughts

---

### 5. Growth System

#### Journey Check-in
- weekly reflection
- admin-defined dynamic questions
- one submission per user per week

#### Saturday Takeaway
- one action step
- one per week

#### Midweek Reflection
- follow-up on the takeaway
- one per week

---

### 6. Announcements
- leader communication
- updates for the group
- published-only visibility for members

---

## Out of Scope
- Social features
- Notifications
- AI-generated content
- Daily Alignment (removed)
- Gamification or performance tracking

---

## Product Structure

The system works as:

Week (time)
↓
Weekly Word (meaning)
↓
User Response:
- Prayer
- Journal
- Growth actions

---

## Final Statement

Growing in Christ is not built to impress.

It is built to:
→ guide people into a consistent, honest, and grace-filled walk with God over time.
